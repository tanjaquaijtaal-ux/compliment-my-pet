### Responsible AI triggers
In some cases, uploading a photo may unexpectedly trigger **Responsible AI restrictions**.

At the moment, the exact cause is not always clear.

**Suggested workaround:**
- Try uploading the same photo again, or
- Use a different photo, or
- Continue the conversation without a photo (this works perfectly)

This was an important learning about how **image input and safety systems** can behave in real-world scenarios.
